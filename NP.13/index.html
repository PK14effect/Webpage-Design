MY HERO

ACADEMIA

Izuku Midoriya

A boy who dreams of becoming a hero. He was born ＂Quirkless＂, but after his hidden inner qualities as a hero were discovered, he inherited the One for All ＂Quirk＂ from All Might. His determination to save others is unusually strong.

Shoto Todoroki

A top-class big shot with both intelligence and physical strength who was admitted to U.A. High School on recommendation. His ＂Quirk＂ is 'Half-Cold Half-Hot', which allows him to unleash freezing from his right side and heat from his left. His father is Endeavor, the current No. 1 pro hero.

Katsuki Bakugo

Deku's childhood friend. He has outstanding confidence in his intelligence, strength, and combat sense, and has a firm belief that 'he will be the No. 1 hero', with a strong desire to rise to the top. His ＂Quirk＂ is 'Explosion', which causes nitroglycerin-like sweat to come from the sweat glands in his palms and explode.

Shota Aizawa

A pro hero who teaches at U.A. High School and is the homeroom teacher of Izuku and his class of 1A. He demands logical thinking and not wasting time, and gives the impression of being strict, but he teaches while closely watching over his students. His ＂Quirk＂ is 'Erasure' which allows him to erase the Quirks of people he sees.

All Might

A hero known as the ＂Symbol of Peace＂, he is the 8th successor to inherit the ＂One for All ＂Quirk＂, which allows him to accumulate and transfer power passed down for generations. After exhausting his powers in a battle with his arch-enemy, All for One, he has now retired as a hero. As a teacher at U.A., he trains and guides Izuku, who he has transferred One for All to.
