MY VILLAIN

ACADEMIA

Tomura Shigaraki

＂Quirk＂: Disintegration
A man who believes in 'destroying everything'. In fact, he is the grandson of Nana Shimura, the 7th generation heir to 'One for All'. He has a past that when he was a child, his family was killed by his ＂quirk＂. He was raised by All for One as his successor, and was awakened during his battle against Re-Destro. He is the supreme leader of the ＂Paranormal Liberation Front＂, a huge force with over 100,000 members.

Dabi

Dabi is a major supporting character and mysterious villain. He was invited to join the League of Villains by a black-market broker. A firm believer in Stain's 'Revival of Heroics' ideology. He is also a member of the organization Vanguard Action Squad. His background is completely unknown.

Himiko Toga

A high school girl who was invited by a black market broker to join the League of Villains. She is wanted by the police as a suspect in a series of blood loss death cases. Her ＂Quirk＂ allows her to transform into another person by ingesting their blood.

Twice

A member of the League of Villains, he is a villain with his entire body clad in black. He's always high energy, and his words and actions are inconsistent. Able to create clones of himself and others with his 'Double' ＂Quirk＂, which allows him to duplicate things.

Mr. Compress

Member of the League of Villains, a villain in a coat, mask, and silk hat. He calls himself an entertainer. Has a ＂Quirk＂ that compresses people and objects into small balls.

Spinner

Shuichi is a young man with a reptile-like appearance, with bright green scales for skin and a face shaped like that of a lizard. His hair is relatively long and swept backward and is a desaturated pink-purple, standing out from the rest of his colors.

Magne

Magne was a woman with quite a wide, muscular build, her hair smooth, shoulder-length, and reddish magenta color, and her rather square jaw covered thinly with facial hair. Her lips were notably big, appearing to be similar to Rikido Sato’s, just slightly more pink-tinted in color, and she always wore triangular, white-framed sunglasses, obscuring her eyes. Along with these, she also wore a dark orange shirt, left unbuttoned to reveal with white v-neck she had on underneath, and blue jeans secured with a belt, plain dark brown espadrilles on her feet.

Muscular

Goto is a large, bulky, and extremely muscular man set wide with short, spiky, and sandy blond hair with small black eyes. He has a large scar going across the left side of his face, his left eye replaced with a prosthetic one, and he has another, smaller scar on the right side of his forehead. As of his appearance after the Paranormal Liberation War, Goto's hair has grown longer and he has developed stubble all over his chin. While facing Izuku for the second time, he crushes and carves a piece of rock to use in place of his left prosthetic eye.

Mustard

A member of the League of Villains, he is a villain that dresses in a gas mask and a gakuran, and has a ＂Quirk＂ that releases and controls toxic gas around himself.

Moonfish

A League of Villains member, he wears a straitjacket, and is a natural-born cold-blooded killer, who was sentenced to death but escaped. His teeth can grow into a blade-like shape and be used to attack and such, with his ＂Quirk＂ that he can operate with ease.

Kurogiri

A villain who plays the role of an advisor and works together with Shigaraki. He has a ＂Quirk＂ that allows him to connect different spaces through his own body, and transport anything.

All For One

After being mortally injured by All Might years ago, All For One's face was entirely made up of scar tissue, extending from above his upper lip and covering his entire head and the back of his neck. As such, he had no visible nose, ears, hair or eyes, though the outlines of his eye sockets were visible. All For One was blind, but used an Infrared Quirk to "see" and he would sense the directions of vibrations in the area around him, similar to echolocation. There is a small hole in each palm of his hands, which is how he steals and transfers Quirks.
